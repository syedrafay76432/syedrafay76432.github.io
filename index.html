Recipe #1

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 cup
Ingredients Quantity
Pumpkin 200gm
Onion 1 medium size
Tomato 1 medium size
Pink salt 1 table spoon
Powdered black pepper 2 pinch
Cream (To decorate 1 table spoon

Steps:
(01) Peel and cut pumpkin tomato onion and garlic
(02) Place all in pressure cooker and cook till two whistles
(03) After releasing pressure place all material in a plate
(04) Cool down properly and blend it in puree
(05) Cook this puree in a pan for two minutes and mix powdered black pepper
(06) Garnish with cream (if you want to garnish) and serve hot

Recipe #2

  • Time:25 minutes
  • Serve:1 plate
    •
    Ingredient Quamtity
    Large sweet potato 1 nose Chicken boneless breast 3-4 nos

    Steps:
    01 Boil/pressure cook the sweet potato.Deskin,mash well and keep aside to cool a bit
    02 Make fork marks/slight cuts with a sharp knife on the chicken fillets
    03 keep the dry spices ready to be used on a plate or a flat surface
    04 Add oil and ginger garlic paste to the dry spices and blend well
    05 Gently coat both sides of the chicken fillets with the spice mix and keep covered for at least 5-7 minutes
    06 Meanwhile start the oven to grill mode to prepare
    07 After marination, place the chicken fillets on the grill rack and grill for around 4-5 minutes on each side
    08 to keep the chicken warm, keep them in the oven till serving
    09 before plating, take the chicken off the grill add a dash of fresh lemon juice over the fillets
    10 Add a pinch of salt to the mashed sweet potato and mix well
    11 slice fresh avocado lengthwise
    12 To serve, place a generous scoop of mashed sweet potato in the center and arrange the chicken fillets around serve accompanied with avocado, lemon and salad of your choice
